Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for a Dhani loan?

Any Indian citizen above 18 years with valid ID and income proof can apply.

What is the minimum loan amount?

You can apply for as little as ₹5,000 and up to ₹10,00,000 depending on your profile.

How soon will I get the funds?

Most approved loans are disbursed within 24 hours after document verification.

How do I repay the loan?

You can set up automatic EMI from your bank account or pay via UPI, net banking or our mobile app.
